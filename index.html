A Family Recipe
Passed Down Generations

Thanusha's Kitchen was born from a simple desire — to share the authentic, soul-warming flavors of traditional Andhra kitchen with every household across India and beyond.

Our pickles are prepared by hand, exactly as our grandmothers taught us. We source raw mangoes from the famous Gir belt, tender gongura leaves fresh from Guntur farms, and aromatic spices directly from Rajahmundry spice markets.

Every batch is sun-dried for the right number of days, seasoned with precise proportions of chilli powder, mustard oil and sea salt — and never touched by preservatives or artificial color.
